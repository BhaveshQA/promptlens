Night Street Photography Prompts: Neon, Rain & Urban Grit

Use reflections, lens choice, ISO grain, and motion language to make night images feel photographed.

Jan 7, 20266 min read

Night street prompts work best when the light source is visible or implied: signage, shop windows, headlights, street lamps, or wet pavement reflections.
