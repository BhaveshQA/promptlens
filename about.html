The Story

PromptLens started in 2024 when I noticed a gap — AI image generation tools were incredibly powerful, but most people had no idea how to write prompts that produced professional-quality results.

I spent months testing prompts across Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion — documenting what worked, what didn't, and exactly why. The result is this site: a living library of prompts organised by photography style, lighting technique, and mood.

Every prompt on PromptLens is tested by a human. Nothing is auto-generated or scraped — every prompt is written, tested, and refined before it goes live. If it doesn't produce a stunning result, it doesn't make the cut.

What I stand for
