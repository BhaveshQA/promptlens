Our Story

PromptLens started in 2024 when a small group of photographers and AI enthusiasts noticed a gap: AI image generation tools were incredibly powerful, but most people had no idea how to write prompts that produced professional-quality results.

We spent months testing thousands of prompts across Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion — cataloguing what worked, what didn't, and why. The result is this site: a living library of prompts organised by photography style, lighting, technique, and mood.

Every prompt on PromptLens is tested by a human. We don't auto-generate or scrape — we write, test, and refine each one before it goes live. If it doesn't produce a stunning result, it doesn't make the cut.

What we stand for

Three principles that guide everything we build.

🎯

Tested, Not Generated

Every prompt is manually tested before publishing. We reject generic AI-written prompt lists — quality over quantity, always.

🆓

Free Forever

PromptLens is and will always be free to use. We sustain the site through advertising, not paywalls or subscriptions.

📸

Photography-First

We focus exclusively on photography styles — not general AI art. That depth means better, more specific prompts for serious creators.
