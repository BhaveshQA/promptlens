💡 Prompt Tips for Better Results
Always specify the camera & lens
Add "shot on Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.4" to your prompt. AI models respond strongly to real camera specs and generate more photorealistic, technically plausible images compared to generic descriptions.
Name the lighting source
Replace "good lighting" with specific sources: "single Rembrandt key light from camera left", "soft overcast sky diffusion", or "golden hour backlight with rim highlight". Lighting specificity transforms the mood entirely.
